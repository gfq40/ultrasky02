Kamilia Kard is an artist and lecturer born in Milan. Her work examines how hyperconnectivity and new forms of online communication have changed and influenced our perception of the human body, gestures, feelings, and emotions. She moves across various media, including printed images, video games, websites, 3D printing, performance, and virtual environments. Her work has been exhibited in galleries, festivals, and institutions both in Italy and internationally. She holds a PhD in Digital Humanities from the University of Genoa and is a professor of Multimedia Communication and Virtual Reality and Complexity Paradigms at the Brera Academy of Fine Arts in Milan.
